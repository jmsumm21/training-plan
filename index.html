🏃
Run Fuelling by Duration
  • Under 75 min — nothing needed. Water only.
  • 75–120 min — 1 gel or 2 medjool dates at 60 min.
  • 2–3 hrs — 30–40g carbs/hr from 45 min. Gels + real food.
  • 3–5 hrs — 40–60g carbs/hr. Boiled potatoes, PB sandwich, rice balls.
  • Race (8–12 hrs) — gels first 3hrs, then real food. Caffeinated gels after 4hrs only.
🥩
Daily Recovery Nutrition
  • 1.8g protein per kg — spread across meals
  • Carb-load night before and morning of long runs
  • Within 30–45 min post-run: protein + carbs (chocolate milk works)
  • Add sodium to post-run meals — replaces sweat losses
  • Tart cherry juice reduces muscle soreness measurably
💧
Hydration
  • 500–700ml per hour on easy runs
  • 600–800ml per hour on long runs and in heat
  • Electrolyte tabs on anything over 90 min
  • Drink to thirst on race day — don't force fluids
  • Pale yellow urine is the target
⚡
Supplements
  • Omega-3 fish oil daily — reduces systemic inflammation
  • Creatine 3–5g daily — aids muscle recovery
  • Collagen + vitamin C before bed — supports tendon repair
  • Beetroot shots 2–3hrs pre-race — nitric oxide boost
  • Avoid alcohol during heavy training weeks
